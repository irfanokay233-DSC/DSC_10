## Crypto Market Inefficiencies

### Overview

This project explores structural inefficiencies in leveraged cryptocurrency markets.

The focus is on how extreme conditions can create temporary distortions in price behavior.

### What I did

- analyzed price behavior under high leverage  
- studied patterns around liquidation events  
- explored how inefficiencies develop and resolve  

### What makes this unique

This project looks at market behavior under stress, which is often overlooked in standard models.

It focuses on dynamics that only appear in extreme environments.

### Why it matters

Understanding these conditions can provide insight into risk and potential opportunities in volatile markets.
