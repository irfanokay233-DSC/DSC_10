## Backtests vs Live Trading Performance

### Overview

This project examines why trading strategies often perform well in backtests but fail to deliver similar results in live environments.

The focus is on identifying and understanding the sources of this gap.

### What I did

- simulated strategies under ideal conditions  
- introduced realistic constraints such as costs and slippage  
- compared performance before and after adjustments  

### What makes this useful

This project goes beyond building strategies and focuses on evaluating their realism.

It highlights common mistakes that lead to overly optimistic results.

### Why it matters

Understanding these distortions is essential for building strategies that work outside of controlled environments.
